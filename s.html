012 | Das Haus in der Mitte der Stadt · 2018 · Ruffinihaus, Munich, GE

 

 

 

4 I 4

<

>

Published in Entkunstung Magazin, Text w. Paula Brücke

Exhibited at Ruffinihaus, Munich (GE)

 

A year after the publication of a text that deals with the “Atelier Brunnenstrasse”, a project by the Berlin-based architect Brandlhuber Arno. the text was decontextualized in an exhibition at the “Ruffinihaus” in Munich. The “Ruffinihaus” was facing a renovation by various German and foreign investors, making the project to a resemblance of the highly speculative real estate economy in Munich. The text was combined with a wooden rip radiator, addressing topics of user-friendliness, renovation and repurposing.

011 | I spy With my Little Eye · A Korpus, 2017 · Polymer, Tallinn, EE

 

 

 

6 I 6

<

>

Materials

 

Perforated Aluminium, Light

 

 

At Polymer, a former USSR toy factory, we were invited to do a site-specific intervention. A 4m duct, once part of the ventilation system, was converted into a column. Not only does it “support” and reorganise the main hall of the factory’s A-corpurs, but also it functions as a lantern to the scarcely lit space. Today the building is used as an artist-run space, hosting studios for local and international artists, and providing space for exhibitions and concerts.

006 | What does your house in the basement look like · The Conversation, 2017

<

>

Materials

 

Perforated Wood, Coated Aluminium, Bolts

 

 

 

A participatory installation at MAMÜ, a basement gallery in Budapest, was shaped by live dialogue performers and listeners of Tilos Rádió’s show. Local residents were invited to call in and share thoughts on the conditions of their basements during a live interview with members of Supra//Struktur. This event gave birth to the structure implemented at the subterranean exhibition space.
